Meiket is a place to create, share, and play in VR spaces. Built on the web so any VR device can access it, Meiket revolutionizes how people share VR experiences. It's a platform made with developers in mind, with built-in tools to create both in and out of VR. And because it can be accessed through a web browser, you can share these experiences - both creating and playing - with anyone you want.
